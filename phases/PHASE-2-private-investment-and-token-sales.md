# Phase 2 — Private Investment & Token Sales

**Goal:** raise enough capital to fund **audits (Spearbit + Trail of Bits)**, finalisation, launch
liquidity, and runway — without compromising the community-aligned tokenomics in `TOKENOMICS.md`.

**Exit criteria:** audit budget + finalisation runway secured; strategic allocations (if any) sized
within the **5% Investor Reserve**; legal/compliance clear; funds in treasury.

> Note on philosophy: `TOKENOMICS.md` §7 states **"No VC round. Investor Reserve (5%) drawn only
> for strategic partners; unsold portion → Community at M24."** This phase is about a *lean,
> strategic* raise to fund the audit and launch — not a traditional VC round. Keep it small and
> aligned, or fund the audit from the public sale proceeds instead (see option B).

---

## 2.1 Decide the funding path

- [ ] **Option A — Strategic private allocation** from the 5% Investor Reserve (50M VIRIO):
      sell a *portion* to strategic partners (exchanges, market makers, ecosystem partners) under
      the `TOKENOMICS.md` vesting (12-mo cliff, 24-mo linear). Unsold → Community at M24.
- [ ] **Option B — Audit-from-public-sale:** skip private investors; bridge-fund the audit from
      founders/treasury and recoup from the public sale (`TOKENOMICS.md`: 50M open sale, $5–15M).
- [ ] **Option C — Hybrid:** small strategic raise for audit + MM relationship, public sale for the rest.
- [ ] Document the decision and its impact on the insider-footprint cap (20% per `TOKENOMICS.md`).

## 2.2 Budget — what the raise must cover

- [ ] **Audits:** Spearbit + Trail of Bits (pre-TGE) — get quotes; this is the primary driver.
- [ ] **Finalisation runway:** dev time to fix audit findings + ship the claim UI (Phase 4).
- [ ] **Launch liquidity:** seed for Uniswap V3 on Ethereum/Base/Arbitrum (LP burnt on deposit).
- [ ] **Listings:** tier-2 CEX at M1 (`TOKENOMICS.md` §7) — fees/market-making.
- [ ] **Legal:** token sale structuring, SAFT/agreements, jurisdiction review.
- [ ] **Ops:** infra for the airdrop engine + dashboard + keepers.
- [ ] Add contingency (audits often surface re-audit needs).

## 2.3 Strategic allocation mechanics (if Option A/C)

- [ ] Size each allocation against the 5% reserve; never exceed it.
- [ ] Vesting via the Phase 1 vesting contract (12-mo cliff, 24-mo linear) — no insider unlocks
      before M6 (`TOKENOMICS.md` §2).
- [ ] Prefer partners who add value: market makers (liquidity), CEXs (listings), ecosystem.
- [ ] Term sheet + SAFT; clear price and unlock terms.
- [ ] **No allowlist conflation:** these are sale agreements, *not* the airdrop eligibility list.

## 2.4 Legal & compliance

- [ ] Jurisdiction + securities review for any private sale.
- [ ] KYC/AML on strategic investors.
- [ ] Entity structure for treasury custody (4-of-7 Safe → DAO at M12).
- [ ] Public-sale legal posture (open sale, no private rounds) reviewed.

## 2.5 Treasury & custody

- [ ] Funds land in the multisig (4-of-7 Safe + 48h timelock).
- [ ] Audit + liquidity budgets ring-fenced.
- [ ] Reporting cadence to align with eventual DAO handover (M12).

## Definition of done

- [ ] Funding path chosen and documented; insider footprint still ≤ 20%.
- [ ] Audit + finalisation + liquidity budgets fully covered.
- [ ] Any strategic allocations papered, KYC'd, and within the 5% reserve with correct vesting.
- [ ] Legal/compliance sign-off; funds secured in treasury.
- [ ] Audit engagement(s) booked → unblocks Phase 3.
